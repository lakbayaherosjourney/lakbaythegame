Lakbay: A Hero's Journey

Lakbay: A Hero's Journey is an RPG and action hack and slash game that follows the story of Kael Datu in the Lanao Lake where a giant called Grohder lived,
and news about Grohder got to the Kingdom of Datu where Kael Datu and Iskar Datu swore to kill the monster.
